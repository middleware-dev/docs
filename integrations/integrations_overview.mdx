Connect your third-party integrations with Middleware. Middleware offers five different types of integrations:  __Cloud__, __Collaboration__, __Databases__,  __Streaming__ and __Telemetry__. Each integration offers unique benefits to help you optimize your team's workflows and extend the capabilities of your Middleware account.

# Benefits

**Improved Collaboration**: Enhance cross-functional collaboration, visibility, and ensure swift incident management throughout your organization.

**Efficient Cloud Management**: Monitor your cloud usage, optimize resource utilization, and proactively manage your cloud environment with cloud integrations.

**Streamlined Database Operations**: Manage, monitor, and optimize database performance by customizing your metric ingestion and visualizing query performance.

**In-Depth Application Insights**: Gain real-time insights into application performance and quickly troubleshoot issues with telemetry integrations.
